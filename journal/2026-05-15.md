## Lessons
- **Don't let cascading dep failures hide:** `pip install -r requirements.txt` rolled back ALL deps when one (`ta`) failed to build. The fix (upgrade setuptools/wheel first) is now documented in learnings.md and should be the first step of any routine that touches Python in the cloud environment.
- **Mean-reversion needs a regime check.** Don't propose mean-reversion against a broken long-term trend in an overbought market — that's two crossed currents at once.
- **NVDA staleness rule (proposed 2026-05-13) is proving its worth.** Setup #1 carried `Approved: YES` for 4 trading days while being unfillable; without the staleness re-check requirement, it would have silently re-armed today on a fresh scan and produced a chase entry $25 above the original zone. Today's resolution to flip back to `STALE` and require a brand-new proposal is the correct outcome.
