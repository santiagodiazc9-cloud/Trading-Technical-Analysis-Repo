### MEDIUM
1. **`requirements-bootstrap.txt:5`** — `setuptools>=65` floor permits CVE-2024-6345 (RCE via `package_index`, fixed 70.0) and CVE-2025-47273 (path traversal, fixed 78.1.1). Fresh `pip install` resolves to a safe version, but cached/offline/reproducible builds could regress. **Fix:** `setuptools>=78.1.1`.
2. **`requirements*.txt` (omission)** — `anthropic` and `requests` are not declared anywhere; they float as transitives. Anthropic SDK family has CVE-2025-49596 (MCP Inspector RCE) — not core SDK calls, but worth pinning. **Fix:** add explicit `anthropic>=0.40.0` and `requests>=2.33.0` to `requirements.txt`.

### LOW
1. **`scripts/daytrader/ml_model.py:127`** — `pickle.load(f)` deserializes trained model artifacts. Local-only path written by the same process, but a compromised `models/` dir gives RCE. **Fix:** `joblib` with `mmap_mode='r'`, or `skops.io`, or SHA-256 manifest pre-check.
2. **`requirements.txt:7`** — `httpx>=0.25.0` floor predates HTTP/2 stream-window DoS hardening. **Fix:** `httpx>=0.27.2`.
3. **`api/main.py:83`, `scripts/daytrader/engine.py:139,163,178`** — `subprocess.run(["python3", ...])` resolves `python3` from `$PATH`. Low risk in launchd/GHA, but unnecessary. **Fix:** use `sys.executable` (already the pattern in `scripts/discord_bot.py`).

### INFO (positive)
1. `.env` properly gitignored (`.gitignore:1`), `git log --all --full-history -- .env` empty, `git ls-files` shows no tracked `.env`. **No remediation.**
2. `/scan` user input in `scripts/discord_bot.py:427` is regex-validated (`^[A-Z.\-]{1,8}$`) AND passed as list-arg to `subprocess.run` (no `shell=True`). Defense in depth. **No remediation.**
