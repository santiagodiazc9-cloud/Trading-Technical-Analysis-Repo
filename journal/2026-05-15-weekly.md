## Strategy Adjustments Made (formalized via ADR)

Three new ADRs written today. **No changes to entry criteria, position sizing, stop methodology, or sector discipline** (CLAUDE.md hard rules 1–15 unchanged). All three new rules govern *setup lifecycle*.

### ADR-0002 — Approved-Setup 2-Day Staleness
An `Approved: YES` setup that has not filled within 2 trading days must be re-evaluated against fresh indicators in the next pre-market routine. Auto-stale → brand-new proposal required. Closes the NVDA-2026-05-08 silent-drift loophole.

### ADR-0003 — Approval-Zone Immutability
Once a setup is published with an entry zone, stop, target, and size, the agent may NOT mutate those parameters intraday. Only Santiago redefines, or the setup auto-stales. Closes the "make the trade work intraday" loophole — preserves the approval gate as a real gate.

### ADR-0004 — Half-Trigger Ledger
Multi-condition re-arm gates with at least one but not all conditions firing get logged in `open_positions.md` + `learnings.md` at the routine where the partial trigger first fires. Subsequent routines inherit the state. Cheap-to-write at midday, expensive-to-reconstruct at EOD or next pre-market.

## Lessons from Week 2

1. **Discipline is only measurable on evaluable days.** A zero-trade week with 80% of routines failing is a 1-of-5 discipline data point, not a "disciplined week." Honest categorization at the weekly review is the only way to avoid mistaking blindness for restraint.
2. **The approval gate is the agent's most important boundary.** When an attractive trade is within a buck of execution all day and improving, the temptation to mutate parameters or self-approve is real. ADR-0003 codifies the right behavior under that pressure.
3. **Multi-day setups need state.** Re-deriving every re-arm condition from scratch at every routine is fine for one setup; unsustainable for three. ADR-0004 makes the partial state explicit and inheritable.
4. **Approvals expire.** ADR-0002 closes the silent-drift loophole that almost executed a 7-day-old NVDA approval at a 12%-outrun price.
5. **Infrastructure is the gating constraint.** Until the cloud routine scheduler is reliably firing all five routines on schedule and Discord credentials are provisioned, the rest of the system is operating with significant degradation.
